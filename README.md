Welcome to **ScentMate**, a dynamic database-driven community platform for fragrance enthusiasts built with PHP. ScentMate is made to help users discover, rate, and show their personal perfume collections. Whether you are a casual wearer or a dedicated "nose", ScentMate connects you with the art of perfumery.

ScentMate offers an immersive experience where users can deep dive into the details of their favourite scent. The site features a **Admin Dashboard** for database management and a sophisticated **Perfume Detail** page. The platform provides a technical look. based on their specific "accord", into terms of sillage, projection and longevity.
# Features ðŸŒŸ
* **Homepage:** Displays a grid of featured perfumes with real-time sorting from A-Z/Z-A and average community ratings.
* **Perfume Profile:** Each frangrance features its "Top 3 Accords", a picture of the respective perfume and technical performance metrics (Sillage, Projection, Longevity) rendered via a star-rating system.
* **Review System:** Logged-in users can submit detailed feedback, including "pros" and "cons" and performance scores, holding a transparent community dialogue.
* **Profile Page:** Users can manage their credentials and select a "favourite fragrance" to showcase on their public profile.
* **Top Rated Leaderboard:** A page that calculates the best performing fragrances based on the average of all user-submitted metrics.
* **Admin Dashboard:** Secure, password-protected command center for admins to add, edit, or delete perfumes, manage brands, and update scent accords.
* **Secure Authentication:** Features secure login systems for both users and administrators using password hashing and session management.
